About Me

I was first introduced to Web Development when I was 21, by my older sister. She studied graphic design and I was amazed by how she was able to manipulate a webpage. She taught me basic CSS and some basic HTML. When I was 26 I studied Intro to Web Dev at Lambda School , full time, and learned HTML and CSS indepth. I love being able to control and design an entire site, as well as the fast paced learning environment and team atmosphere. I enjoy collaborating with others and learning from people who have more experience than I do, because it allows me room for growth.

I also have a background in a multitude of different subjects. Learning is my passion.
